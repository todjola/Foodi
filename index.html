How it works

Your daily dose of health in 3 simple steps

01

Share Your Preferences and Dietary Needs

All you need to do is tell us your likes, dislikes, and dietary requirements. Whether you’re into low-carb, vegan, high-protein, or gluten-free options, our advanced AI takes your input and creates a 100% personalized meal plan just for you. No more guesswork—just meals that perfectly match your taste and health goals.

02

Approve your weekly meal plan

Take charge of your weekly menu with complete flexibility. Each week, you’ll receive a proposed meal plan tailored to your preferences. Don’t like an ingredient? No problem—swap it out! Want to try something new? Add your own favorite recipes or browse our chef-curated menu for inspiration.It makes it easy to tweak and approve your meals so you’re always excited about what’s coming next.

03

Receive meals at convenient time

Delicious, healthy meals delivered to your door—effortlessly. Once your menu is set, our team of expert chefs prepares your meals using the freshest ingredients. Each dish is cooked with precision and care to ensure the highest quality. Then, we deliver your meals at the time that’s most convenient for you. Change your delivery schedule or address anytime—it’s all about making healthy eating fit seamlessly into your lifestyle.
